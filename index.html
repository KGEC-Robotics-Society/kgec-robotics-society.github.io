Our Domains

Artificial Intelligence

The ML and OpenCV specialists of our team have honed their skills through regular practice and have read through tones of research papers and books to make sure they have enough knowledge to compete in any given circumstance. They have made sure their models make extremely precise predictions and detected extensive arenas. They are the heart and soul of our team without whom the team would be unoperational.

Content Writers

The content writers and PR specialists of our team are the ones providing you with information that you can easily comprehend. They are the ones attracting you with their taglines and the ones forming a bridge between you and our team. Their role is as important as any other member of the team as they need to make sure they have sound knowledge of all other domains in order to be able to write and demonstrate our projects to you.

Graphics Designing and
Video Editing

The graphic designers and the video editors create engaging and on-brand graphics/videos to optimize user experience. Graphic designers attract the audience using images, color and typography. They are responsible for the visual hierarchy, layout and aesthetic of visual contents. Video editors create new video contents by manipulating and rearranging video shots based on requirement. Editing is an important step which makes sure the video flows such a way that the videos tell a story and provide information to the audience. So good graphic designing and video editing capture the attention of audience and make them understand what the institution has to offer.

IoT and Electronics

Electronics, branch of physics and electrical engineering that deals with the emission, behaviour, and effects of electrons and with electronic devices. The Internet of Things (IoT) describes the network of physical objects—“things”—that are embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies for the purpose of connecting and exchanging data with other devices and systems over the internet. Here in IOT and electronics domain we deal with both of them. We have worked on various projects that are based on them.

Mechatronics and Robot design

The most interesting and the most initial phase of any hardware based project we work on, Mechatronics and the Robot Design Team make sure that our Hardware is designed and made to work with maximum efficiency. They are the ones designing and creating what you see and what you experience through our videos and content. This is an essential step that makes sure that our robots have been designed with utmost precision and thought has been put for further upgradations.

Web Devolopement

With the growing number of web-enabled devices, web design and development and cloud computing carry heavy importance to attain easy access to data effortlessly,Web design and development describes the process of creating a website to engage with audience where web design determines the layout and look of the site while web development determines how it functions and stores information. A good website is innovative, beautiful, easy to use and provides opportunities for users to gain useful information and build a relationship with one's institution. Cloud computing provides various computing services like servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics, and intelligence—over the internet. Cloud computer service can reduce cost of IT systems, has collaboration efficiency and it is scalable.
